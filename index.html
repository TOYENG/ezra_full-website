Welcome to my 24/7 Auto Repair Shop

The objective of TOYENG 24/7 Auto Repair Shop is to provide high quality, convenient and comprehensive auto repair at low cost. The most important aspect of our business is trust. It is the goal of our firm to have 100% customer satisfaction in regards to quality, friendliness, time to completion and to discover new ways to exceed the expectations of our clients.

While repairing our customers vehicles we will use only quality parts and we strive to always have on hand the most current information available for that vehicle. We will continue to acquire the most current diagnostic equipment and hand held computers that will link your vehicle information with our trained technicians. We at TOYENG 24/7 Auto Repair Shop will strive to continue the practice of quality automotive care because "The Measure of our Success is based on the Satisfaction of our Customers".

We are a full service automotive repair facility servicing business and residential customers within the City of Baguio and surrounding communities. We offer quality, professional and timely service on all mechanical repairs for import and domestic vehicles as well as light duty & medium truck. Whether it be Computer Diagnostics to check out an electrical problem and/or running condition or planned maintenance inspections to determine the condition of your vehicle, our ASE certified technicians will service your vehicle (s) with complete satisfaction.
