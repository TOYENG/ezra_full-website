About Us

TOYENG 24/7 Auto Repair Shop was originally formed in June of 1987 and operated out of a 2 bay brick garage in Tabuk,Kalinga,Phillipines . Staffed with 4 technicians, one which was Mr. Toyeng himself and a partner helping out part-time doing bookkeeping and other office duties.

In October of 1988, Mr. Toyeng moved his business 12 miles to its present location in Baguio. (With 10 service bays, an office and a customer waiting area) Over the last 35 years Mr. Toyeng has steadily built TOYENG 24/7 Auto Repair Shop by taking 100% ownership and adding an additional service bay plus a 4 post alignment rack and 4 post service bay for a total of 10 services bays.

Along with the additional space Mr. Toyeng now staffs 4 full time ASE certified technicians along with himself and a full-time office manager. During the last 35 years TOYENG 24/7 Auto Repair Shop has been in business our customer base has grown to over 2700 customers and many Fleet accounts.
